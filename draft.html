Illegal Deportation on
Long Island?

By: Joshua Cohen

On November 20th, lawyers from the Central American Refugee Center, a Long Island based immigration services center, and the Hofstra Law Clinic filed suit against Nassau County in New York Supreme Court for illegal practices in detaining immigrants.
Following the traffic stop of Denis Guerra Guerra in August for failing to signal when changing lanes, a Nassau County police officer discovered a pending order of removal from ICE, the United States Immigration and Customs Enforcement Agency and took him under custody which resulted in his deportion to El Salvador.
