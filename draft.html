Illegal Deportation on
Long Island?

By: Joshua Cohen

On November 20th, lawyers from the Central American Refugee Center(CARC), a Long Island based immigration services center, and the Hofstra Law Clinic filed suit against Nassau County in New York Supreme Court for illegal practices in detaining immigrants.
They claim the Nassau County Police Department has violated New York State law by detaining people on warrants issued by the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement Agency...
even without authorities having any proof
that the person has commited a crime.

Image Credit: Long Island Wins


During a routine traffic stop in August for faling to signal when changing lanes, Denis Guerra Guerra was one of the allegedly illegal detainees who was arrested and later deported when it was discovered he had a 10-year old pending order of removal from ICE.
Following the traffic stop of Denis Guerra Guerra in August for failing to signal when changing lanes, a Nassau County police officer discovered a pending order of removal from ICE, the United States Immigration and Customs Enforcement Agency and took him under custody which resulted in his deportion to El Salvador.
